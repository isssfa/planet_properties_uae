We are a RERA certified property consultancy & channel partners for A and B category builders across India. We act as advisors and sales professionals, rather than mere brokers. There is a massive difference between the two. A broker is one who acts as a link between the buyer and the seller and takes a commission from both parties. Channel/ Marketing partners are consultants who give researched insights & feedback about the real estate dynamics to the client. As they separate wheat from the chaff, we do due diligence and filter out and cherry pick the properties that qualify our stringent criteria and offer only those properties to our clients.. The selling services are designed but are not limited to a pre-agreed duration, for which we receive recognition & remuneration from the developer for the services rendered but nothing is charged from the buyer for the properties bought through Builders/ Developers.

On the other hand, the buyer or investor receives free services such as site visits, pick and drop, loan assistance, post-sales services, etc. This marketing technique means that the industry is getting more organized and transparent. The buyer can rely on us who make the property search experience more reachable and hassle-free.

Having a channel partner as a consultant eases the processes of looking for a property a lot more and also increases options for customers. The customers can engage with channel partners after extensive diligence and let them play an advisory role by guiding them appropriately.
